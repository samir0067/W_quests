About Us

From the twenty or so acrobats that made up the Groupe Wild Circus at its beginnings in 1984, the group has completely reinvented the circus arts and has become a world leader in the world of stage shows. Based in Strasbourg, France, the French organization has been a source of wonder for more than 190 million spectators thanks to performances in nearly 450 cities and 60 countries. The Wild Circus Group currently has more than 4,000 employees, including 1,400 artists, from some 50 countries.

The Wild Circus Group develops content for a wide range of daring projects. In addition to producing internationally renowned shows, the organization has expanded its creative approach to include a wide variety of forms of entertainment, including multimedia productions, immersive experiences, theme parks and special events. Beyond its various creations, Groupe Wild Circus aims to positively influence people, communities and the planet through its most important tools: creativity and art.
